      <a name="libregs"></a> <font color="#336633" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><strong>Library        Regulations</strong></font> </p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">It is stated in the 
        Student Charter that each student in the Institute will be treated as 
        an adult and, as such, are expected to behave in a responsible adult manner, respecting 
        the rights of others. This is further emphasised in the Appropriate Behaviour 
        Charter. This, of course, also applies in the Library and the following 
        regulations aim to ensure that the Library is a comfortable environment 
        for all, which is conducive to study.</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">You must hold a 
          current Institute staff/student ID card to enter the Library, use the 
          facilities and to borrow Library materials. This card must be produced 
        if requested by a member of Library staff.</font>        
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">There are designated group study, quiet study and silent study areas in the Library. Noise levels are self-monitoring e.g. if a user is talking in the silent study area, then it is up to the users who have been disturbed to ask that user to move to a group study area. Anyone who continues to disturb 
          others can be asked to leave the Library. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">There must be no 
          eating or drinking (apart from water in pull-up top bottles) in the 
          Library. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Talking on mobile 
          phones, or phones ringing in the Library, is strictly forbidden. They must be set to silent 
          mode. There is an on-the-spot fine of &euro;5 if a mobile rings or if 
          a student is talking on their mobile. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If you wish to 
          borrow from the Library you must bring the item and your ID card to 
          the Issue Desk, or use the self-service system. The self-service system can also be used to return books. There is a security system 
          in operation in the Library, so before leaving, ensure that all items 
          have been checked out properly, otherwise a fine of &euro;5 will be 
          imposed.</font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Users have full 
          responsibility for all Library materials borrowed on their ID card. 
          </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">An item must be 
          returned immediately if recalled by the Librarian. Failure to return 
          Library materials will initially result in a fine, then suspension of 
          borrowing rights and the replacement cost of the materials being incurred 
          by the user. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Theft or the defacing 
          of Library property is a serious offence and will be dealt with in the 
          strictest possible manner. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Study places may 
          not be reserved in the Library. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">External users 
          of the Library must first consult with the Librarian for details of 
          membership. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">It is most important 
          to comply with Copyright regulations. Please observe notices at all 
          photocopiers. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Internet access 
          is available to all students of the Institute, however anyone found 
          to be abusing this facility e.g. accessing inappropriate sites, will 
          have their access denied. </font> 
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><strong>NB.</strong> All e-mail correspondence from the Library is to student e-mail accounts (e.g. B00000000@student.itb.ie). There is no correspondence to any other e-mail address. It is students' responsibility to check their student e-mail regularly. </font>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Library users who 
        breach any of the above regulations will be liable to a withdrawal of 
        services and other disciplinary measures.<br>
        </font> </p>

      <p align="center"><a name="faq"></a><font color="#336633" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" class="green"><strong>Frequently 
        Asked Questions</strong></font> </p>
      <p><font color="#336633" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">How 
        many books can I borrow?</font><font color="green" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        </font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Full time and 
        part time students may borrow up to 6 books at any one time. Postgraduate 
        students may borrow up to 10.</font></p>
      <p><font color="#336633" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Can 
        books be renewed?</font><font color="green" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        </font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Books may be 
        renewed as often as you like, provided nobody else has reserved them and they are not 
        overdue.</font></p>
      <p><font color="#336633" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If 
        the Library does not hold a book, can it be got elsewhere?</font><font color="green" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        </font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Yes. Ask at 
        the Circulation Desk about an <a href="libserv.html#illoans"><font color="#336633">Inter-Library 
        Loan</font></a>.</font></p>
      <p><font color="#336633" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">What 
        are the fines for overdue books?</font><font color="green" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        </font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Long Loans (2 
        weeks) - 40c per day.<br>
        Short Loans (1 week) - 70c per day.<br>
        Day Loans - <span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-IE" lang="EN-IE">ï¿½1 
        per day.</span></font></p>
      <p><font color="#336633" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">What 
        are the fines for overdue laptops?<br>
      </font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If overdue, laptops are charged at <span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-IE" lang="EN-IE">ten euro per day, or part of a day. <br>
        <br>
        <font color="#336633">Where can I do photocopying?</font><font color="#008000"><br>
            </font>There are two student photocopiers in the Library. Students can top up their credit using a credit or debit card. Please see the information sheet at the Student Information Desk or top up <a href="/cashless"><font color="#336633">here</font></a>. </span></font> <font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Full instructions on their use  is posted on the walls above the copiers.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><font color="#336633">Where can I do printing?<br> 
        </font><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-IE">There are three student printers in the Library, one of which prints in colour. Please see the information sheet at the Student Information Desk or top up <a href="/cashless"><font color="#336633">here</font></a>.  </span> Full instructions on their use  is posted on the walls above the printers. <span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-IE" lang="EN-IE"><br>

            <font color="#336633">Can  DVDs be borrowed?</font><font color="#008000"><br>
